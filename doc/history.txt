Kitserver HISTORY notes
==========================

30-03-2024
----------
v13.4.0.0
- support: PES2013 demo, PES2013 v1.03, PES2013 v1.04
- added fserv: assign face/hair (from GDB) to any player you want.
- added kserv: assign kits (from GDB) to any team you want.
- added sides: allow free side selection during matches.
- added afs2fs: read & write bins from personalized path
- added speeder: adjust the game speed (increase/decrease)
- added lodmixer: player and referee LODs, automatic AR-correction, 
  custom resolution selection, picture quality overwrite.
