Kitserver HISTORY notes
==========================

30-03-2024
----------
v13.4.0.0
- support: PES2013 demo, PES2013 v1.03, PES2013 v1.04
- added afs2fs: manage AFS (.img) game content using custom files and folders.
- added kserv: assign your kits (from GDB) to any team you want.
- added fserv: assign your face/hair (from GDB) to any player you want.
- added lodmixer: misc visual tweaks, fine-tune LOD for players and referees.
- added speeder: adjust the game speed (increase/decrease).
- added sides: allows to freely select sides during non-exhibition matches.
